天宾自行车有限公司

关于我们

Born for spoting.

河北天宾自行车制造有限公司，始建厂于1994年，原河北邱县自行车第一配件厂，于2009年成立河北天宾自行车制造有限公司，我公司位于中国河北省南部：冀、鲁、豫交界处。东临106国道，西临107国道，北临邢清高速，毗邻于中国最大自行车零件城，钢铁资源丰富。

Hebei day bin bicycle manufacturing co., LTD., set up factory in 1994, the original qiu county in hebei province first bicycle accessories factory, founded in 2009 in hebei day bin bicycle manufacturing co., LTD., our company is located in the south of China's hebei province, hebei, shandong, henan at the junction. 106 national highway in the east, west is near to 107 national road, north near XingQing highway, adjacent to China's largest bicycle parts city, steel is rich in resources.
